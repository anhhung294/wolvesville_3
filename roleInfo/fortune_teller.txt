Role Description
In each game, you have 2 cards to give to other players. The players you give cards to can use these cards to reveal their roles.

Interactions
When Fortune Teller selects someone, they will receive the card immediately and can use it once anytime.
Fortune Teller can give cards whenever they want. It doesn't have to be the first night.
If Fortune Teller Dies, selected players will still get their cards and can use them.
