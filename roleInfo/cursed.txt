Role description
The Cursed begins the game on the village team. If the cursed is attacked by the werewolves, it does not die but becomes a regular Werewolf. The cursed can also be protected from the bite just like a regular attack.

Win conditions
The Cursed wins with the village if it remains a cursed until the end of the game. However, if it becomes a werewolf, it wins with the wolves.
