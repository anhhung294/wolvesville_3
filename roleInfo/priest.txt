Role description
Once per game, the Priest can throw Holy Water at one player. The Priest can only do this during the day. If the water is thrown at a Werewolf (excluding Sorcerer), the Werewolf will die, and the role of the priest will be revealed. If that player is not a Werewolf, the Priest dies.

The Priest cannot kill Solo Killers.

Win conditions
The Priest wins with the village.
