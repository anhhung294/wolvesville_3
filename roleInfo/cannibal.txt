Role description
Each night the Cannibal gets a skull. The cannibal can decide whether to use their skulls to kill players, or to save them up for another night. The maximum is 5 skulls.

Win Condition
The Cannibal wins if they are the last player alive.

Interactions
If Cannibal tries to eat someone who is protected, they won't kill them but will still loose a skull.
If Cannibal tries to eat a protector along with the player they protect, they will only kill the protector.[2]
If Cannibal eats Headhunter's target at the same time as Headhunter, Headhunter will loose.[3]
If Cannibal is nightmared, they will still obtain an additional skull during the night.[4]
